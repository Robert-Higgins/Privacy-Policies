### 1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and disclosed by Tiggrat Studio ("I", "me", or "my") when you use my mobile game "Letter Drop!" (the "Game"). I am a solo developer committed to protecting your privacy.

The Game is free to download and play, with optional in-app purchases and advertisements.

### 2. Information I Collect
### 2.1 Information You Provide
No account registration or personal information is required to play the Game

If you make in-app purchases, payment information is processed by Google Play Store (subject to their privacy policy)

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
Game Progress Data: Your gameplay progress, unlocked content, level completion, and high scores

Google Play Services: When connected to the internet, certain game data (achievements, leaderboard scores) may be saved to your Google Play Games profile

Local Storage: Most game data is stored locally on your device

### 3. Third-Party Services
The Game uses the following third-party services:

### 3.1 Google AdMob
Displays advertisements within the Game

AdMob may collect device information and usage data for personalized advertising

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings

Review Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### 3.2 Google Play Services
Handles in-app purchases through Google Play Billing

Manages Google Play Games achievements and leaderboards

Processes payment information (I do not have access to your payment details)

Review Google Play Terms: https://play.google.com/about/play-terms

### 3.3 Google Play Games
Stores your game achievements and leaderboard scores

Requires internet connection to sync this data

You can manage your Google Play Games data through your Google account settings

### 4. Data Storage and Retention
Game progress is primarily stored locally on your device

Google Play Games data is stored on Google's servers

I do not maintain separate servers for user data

Data synced to Google Play Games will be retained according to Google's policies

### 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure
I do not:

Sell or rent your personal information

Share your data with third parties for marketing purposes

Collect personal information beyond what's described in this policy

Data may be disclosed only:

To comply with legal obligations

To protect my rights or safety

As required by third-party services (Google Play, AdMob)

### 6. Children's Privacy
The Game is not specifically directed at children under 13. While it's a word game suitable for general audiences, I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information, contact me immediately.

### 7. International Data Transfers (GDPR Compliance)
For users in the European Economic Area (EEA):

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data

Data is processed with your consent or for legitimate interests (game functionality)

You can withdraw consent by uninstalling the Game

For data stored with Google services, please contact Google directly for GDPR requests

### 8. Your Rights and Choices
Ad Personalization: Control through your device or Google account settings

Data Deletion: Uninstalling the Game removes locally stored data

Google Play Data: Manage through your Google account settings

In-app Purchases: Manage through Google Play Store settings

I implement reasonable security measures, but no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. Game data is primarily stored locally on your device.
